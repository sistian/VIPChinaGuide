Who We Are Looking For

We welcome guides from all 21 cities we cover. Whether you are a seasoned expert or an enthusiastic newcomer, we want to hear from you.

  • ✓ Licensed Tour Guide — You must hold a valid national tour guide license (导游证).
  • ✓ English Proficiency — Able to communicate clearly with international travelers. Expert or Escort level both welcome.
  • ✓ Passionate & Knowledgeable — You love your city and enjoy sharing its history, culture, and hidden gems.
  • ✓ Professional & Reliable — Punctual, responsible, and committed to delivering excellent customer experiences.
  • ✓ Local Resident — You live in or near the city you want to guide in. Deep local knowledge is essential.
