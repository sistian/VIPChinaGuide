Privacy Policy & Terms

How we protect your data and what you agree to when using VIPChinaGuide.

Privacy Policy

At VIPChinaGuide, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number) when you book
  • Booking details (dates, destinations, preferences)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe, PayPal, Alipay, WeChat Pay)
  • Usage data (pages visited, interactions with our site)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your bookings
  • To communicate with you about your trip
  • To improve our services and website experience

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Payment information is never stored on our servers — it is processed directly by our payment partners.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: You can update your information at any time
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data

Terms of Service

By using VIPChinaGuide, you agree to the following terms:

Booking & Cancellation

  • All bookings must be made at least 3 days in advance
  • Full refund for cancellations 7+ days before the tour date
  • 50% refund for cancellations 3-7 days before the tour date
  • No refund for cancellations within 3 days (rescheduling allowed within 30 days)

Guide Services

  • All guides are licensed by the Chinese National Tourism Administration
  • Guide fees do not include entrance tickets, meals, or transportation
  • If a guide is unavailable, we will provide a replacement of equal or higher level

Liability

VIPChinaGuide acts as a booking platform connecting travelers with licensed guides. We are not liable for incidents during the tour, but we will assist in resolving any disputes. We strongly recommend all travelers purchase travel insurance.
