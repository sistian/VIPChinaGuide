Traveler Essentials

Practical tips to make your China tour smooth and worry-free

✅

What's Included

Private English-speaking guide, basic travel insurance purchase support, and 24/7 customer service. Entrance tickets, meals, transportation, and gratuities are not included unless specified.

🛡️

Free Cancellation

Cancel for free up to 24 hours before your tour. We understand travel plans change, so you can reschedule once without extra fees.

📱

WhatsApp Confirmation

After payment, your booking voucher will be ready instantly. Send it to our WhatsApp support for a quick secondary confirmation and direct guide matching.

🎒

What to Bring

Bring your passport for insurance verification, comfortable walking shoes, a power bank, and a VPN-free messaging app like WhatsApp for easy contact.

💳

Secure Payment

Pay via PayPal or Alipay QR code. Your payment reference is linked to your order ID automatically. No credit card details are stored on our site.

🌐

Internet in China

WhatsApp works in China with a VPN. We recommend renting a pocket WiFi or buying a local SIM card. We can help arrange this before your tour.
