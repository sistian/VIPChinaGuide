About 时间语录® (Shijian Yulu)

"时间语录" (Shijian Yulu), which translates to "Time Quotes" or "Chronicles of Time," is the registered trademark of Wuhan City Zhierchong Culture and Tourism Co., Ltd. (武汉市之二虫文旅有限公司). The four characters are rendered in traditional Song typeface (宋体), reflecting our commitment to preserving and sharing China's rich cultural heritage.

The ® symbol indicates that "时间语录" is a legally registered trademark with the China National Intellectual Property Administration (CNIPA), protected under Chinese trademark law.

This trademark represents our dedication to providing authentic, high-quality tour guide services that connect travelers with the timeless stories, history, and culture of China.
